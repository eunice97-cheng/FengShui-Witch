Tai Sui · 5 Yellow 太岁 · 五黄

Location: South (157.5° – 202.5°)

Understanding this energy: In 2026, the Grand Duke resides in the South, accompanied by the 5 Yellow star of calamity. This does not mean "do not enter" — rather, avoid heavy construction, drilling, or sitting with back to South. The energy is dormant but intense; stillness is protective. Salt water absorbs earth toxicity. Replace every lunar month.

Traditional Remedy: White ceramic jar filled with sea salt, 6 old coins, topped with water. Do not cover. Let salt crystallize naturally. Place Pi Xiu facing outward for wealth protection.

Location: North (322.5° – 37.5°)

Understanding this energy: Three Killings arise from the annual triad. In 2026, North carries risk of loss — wealth, health, relationships. This is not fatalistic; it calls for awareness.
